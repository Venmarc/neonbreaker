# Neon Breaker

Neon Breaker is an exciting, fast-paced brick breaker game featuring vibrant neon graphics and engaging gameplay mechanics.

## Features

- **Arcade Mode**: 10 progressive levels that challenge your skills.
- **Endless Mode**: Infinite looping stages with increasing difficulty. 
- **Dynamic Graphics**: Stunning neon aesthetics and particle effects.

## How to Play

### Controls
- **Move Paddle**: Mouse / Arrow Keys / Drag
- **Launch Ball**: Spacebar / Up Arrow / Tap Top of Screen
- **Dash**: Shift / Double Tap Bottom of screen
- **Aim Sticky Ball**: Hold Down Arrow key while the ball is stuck to aim your next shot.

### Power-Ups
- **Enlarge (Green)**: Expands paddle width for 10 seconds.
- **Multiball (Orange)**: Spawns extra balls instantly!
- **Laser (Red)**: Fires a massive beam after a 2-second charge.
- **Lightning (Yellow)**: Triggers chain brick destruction for 5 seconds.
- **Cluster (Light Red)**: Creates explosive shrapnel hits for 5 seconds.
- **Shield (Blue)**: Saves the ball from death for 10 seconds.
- **Sticky (Green)**: Catch and aim the ball for 10 seconds.
- **Heart (Pink)**: Grants an extra life!

Good luck and aim for the high score!
